<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Customize Your Audio Experience">

Managing the **nhl 27 soundtrack** is straightforward, but many players overlook the depth of customization available. You can tailor the audio to suit your specific needs, whether you want a pure hockey simulation or a music-heavy arcade experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Audio Settings">
    From the main menu, navigate to the "Settings" tab and select "Audio." Here you will find sliders for Master Volume, Music Volume, and Sound Effects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Manage the EA Trax Playlist">
    Enter the "EA Trax" sub-menu. You will see a full list of all licensed songs. Use the toggle switch next to each song to enable or disable it from the rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Presentation Settings">
    Decide if you want the licensed music to play during actual gameplay (stadium speakers) or only in the menus. Many competitive players prefer disabling music during games to hear on-ice cues better.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Commentary and Crowd Levels">
    Balance the soundtrack with the play-by-play commentary. If the music is too loud, it may drown out important tactical information provided by the announcers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
