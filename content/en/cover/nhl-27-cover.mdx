### Celebrini's Historic Rise to the Cover

Celebrini's selection for the cover is a direct reflection of his historic 2025-26 season with the San Jose Sharks. Selected first overall in the 2024 draft, the young phenom set a franchise record with an astonishing 115 points, including 45 goals. He single-handedly carried the Sharks' offensive production, scoring 56 more points than any of his teammates.

Beyond his NHL achievements, Celebrini dominated on the international stage. He was named the IIHF Male Player of the Year for the 2025-26 season, largely due to his brilliant performance for Team Canada in the Winter Olympics. Celebrini led the Olympic tournament with five goals and ten points in just six games, helping Canada secure a silver medal in a thrilling final against Team USA. Celebrini, 20, is featured on both editions of the game, as detailed in the [ESPN cover reveal report](https://www.espn.com/nhl/story/_/id/49358795/sharks-macklin-celebrini-ea-sports-nhl-27-cover), making him the youngest player ever featured as a cover athlete for the series.
