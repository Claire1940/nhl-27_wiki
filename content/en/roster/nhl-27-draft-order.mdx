In NHL 27, the default draft order is determined by the final standings of the preceding season. The 16 teams that miss the Stanley Cup Playoffs are entered into the draft lottery, where the bottom-dwelling teams have the highest statistical probability of landing the coveted first overall pick. The remaining 16 playoff teams are ordered based on when they were eliminated and their regular-season point totals, culminating in the Stanley Cup Champion picking 32nd.
