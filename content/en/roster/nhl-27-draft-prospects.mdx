<Section icon={Trophy} title="Be A Pro: Rising Through the Rankings">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Identifying Gems and Avoiding Busts">

Not every highly-rated prospect becomes a star. The **nhl 27 draft prospects** pool includes "Gems"—players whose true potential is higher than their initial ranking—and "Busts"—players who will never reach their projected ceiling. Identifying these requires looking at specific attribute clusters during the scouting process.

A prospect with high "Offensive Awareness" and "Poise" but a lower overall rating is a classic "Gem" candidate. These players tend to simulate better in Franchise Mode and develop their physical attributes naturally as they age.

| Indicator | Gem Sign | Bust Warning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Awareness** | 80+ in both Off/Def | Below 70 in primary role |
| **Skating** | High Acceleration/Agility | Low Balance/Endurance |
| **Character** | High Poise and Discipline | Frequent Penalties/Low Poise |
| **Consistency** | Consistent production in stats | Wildly fluctuating monthly stats |
