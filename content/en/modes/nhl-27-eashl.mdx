<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Team Tactics and Synergy">

In high-level play, individual skill can only carry a team so far. True dominance on the ice requires deep tactical coordination and build synergy between teammates. When constructing your club's roster, you must ensure that your player builds work together to create mismatches against the opposing defense.

| Skater A | Skater B | Tactical Synergy | Recommended Mode |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Sniper | Playmaker | Elite rush offense, quick one-timers | 3v3 or 6v6 |
| Power Forward | Sniper | Heavy forecheck, net-front screens | 6v6 |
| Two-Way Forward | Offensive D-man | Safe defensive coverage during rushes | 3v3 |
| Defensive D-man | Playmaker | Clean breakouts, lockdown net defense | 6v6 |

When executing an offensive rush, the Playmaker should carry the puck to draw the defenders' attention, opening up passing lanes for the Sniper. Meanwhile, a Power Forward or Grinder should drive hard to the net, screening the goaltender and fighting for rebound opportunities. On defense, players must maintain strict positional discipline, avoiding the temptation to chase the puck carrier into the corners.
