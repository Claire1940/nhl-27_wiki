<Section icon={Users} title="Roster Management and Trade Mechanics">

Roster management in **nhl 27 gm connected** functions differently than the traditional GM modes of the past. The system is built more like a "playable fantasy league," where player movement is streamlined for online efficiency. One of the most significant changes is the removal of No-Move Clauses (NMC), allowing for greater trade flexibility between friends.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Roster Load">
    Load a default or custom roster. Use custom rosters to place legendary players like Wayne Gretzky on specific teams to balance the league.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Trade Block">
    List your players on the trade block. Because there is no AI interaction for trades, you must negotiate directly with other human GMs via the league's communication tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Commissioner Review">
    Once a trade is agreed upon, it enters a "Pending" state. The league commissioner must approve the deal before players are moved between rosters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Free Agent Claims">
    Instead of standard negotiations, free agency uses a "Claim" system. You place a bid amount (e.g., $9.5M) on a player, and the highest bidder wins the claim.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Roster Continuity">
While you can play multiple seasons, be aware that for the 2026 launch, player aging and retirement do not carry over. This means Sidney Crosby will remain his starting age and rating regardless of how many seasons your league completes.
</Callout>

| **Potential** | Hidden/Not applicable in Year 1 |
| **Free Agency** | Waiver-style claim system |
| **Trades** | Human-only (No AI teams) |

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Navigating Known Limitations">

While the return of **nhl 27 gm connected** is a major milestone, the first iteration in the new engine comes with specific limitations that players should understand before starting a long-term league.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Missing Progression">
    - **No Player Aging**: Rookies don't get better; vets don't retire.
    - **Static Stats**: Season stats do not currently influence future ratings.
    - **Development**: Focus is on user skill rather than RPG-style growth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Restrictions">
    - **No AI Trading**: You cannot trade with CPU-controlled teams.
    - **Placeholder Teams**: Teams without a human user cannot be played against in most custom formats.
    - **Drafting**: Entry drafts for new prospects are limited in the launch version.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Future Updates">
EA Sports has confirmed that Connected Franchise is a multi-year project. Features like player progression and a full entry draft are prioritized for future title updates or the subsequent NHL 28 release. Use the official feedback portal to let developers know which features your league needs most.
</Callout>

| Limitation | Impact on Gameplay | Workaround |
