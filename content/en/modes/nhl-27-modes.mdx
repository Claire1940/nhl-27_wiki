<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: Team Identity and AI">

In **nhl 27 modes**, how you play with your team matters more than ever. The introduction of authentic team playbooks means that choosing a team is now a strategic decision rather than just a roster choice. These playbooks are powered by **NHL EDGE** positional data, reflecting how teams actually behave in the offensive and defensive zones.

| AI System | Primary Function |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Cohesive Team AI** | Defenders step up at the blue line and maintain better gap control |
| **Contextual Shifts** | Teams adapt tactics based on score, time remaining, and momentum |
| **Cycle vs. Shoot** | Defines whether a club looks for the perfect pass or hammers the net |
| **Pressure Adjust** | AI teammates hustle more effectively to close down exit lanes |

Cohesive Team AI ensures that your teammates are more aware of the game's flow. If you are protecting a lead late in the third period, your AI defenders will clog the neutral zone and play with a defensive focus. Conversely, if a team is trailing, they will stretch the ice and take more risks to create scoring opportunities.

<Callout type="warning" title="Strategic Awareness">
Trailing teams in the third period will play with "added desperation." Expect aggressive forechecking and more cross-crease pass attempts from the AI.
