<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start Your Online Franchise">

Setting up your first **NHL 27 online franchise** is a straightforward process, but it requires careful planning to ensure long-term league stability. Follow these steps to get your league off the ground.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="League Creation">
    Navigate to the Connected Franchise menu and select "Create League." Choose between a private league for friends or a public league for the community.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define the Rules">
    Set your season length (full 82 games or shortened), playoff format, and difficulty settings. This is where you toggle trade logic and salary cap rules.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invite GMs">
    Use the league ID or invite system to bring in up to 31 other players. You can assign teams or host a "Fantasy Draft" to build rosters from scratch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage the Calendar">
    As the Commissioner, you control when the league advances. Set a "Sim Speed" that works for everyone, ensuring games are played on time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
