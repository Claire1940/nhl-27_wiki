<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Enable and Setup NHL 27 Crossplay">

Setting up your console for cross-platform action is straightforward. By default, **nhl 27 crossplay** is enabled to ensure the best possible matchmaking experience. However, players have the option to toggle this setting if they prefer to stick to their native platform.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Settings">
    From the main menu, navigate to the **Settings** tab and select **Online Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Toggle Crossplay">
    Locate the **Cross-Platform Play** option. Ensure it is set to **Enabled** to participate in the larger matchmaking pool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Link EA Account">
    Go to the **EA Account** section to verify your email and ID. This ID is what you will share with friends on other consoles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Add Friends">
    Open the **Social Hub** (usually by pressing a trigger or dedicated button), search for your friend's EA ID, and send a friend request.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Start a Session">
    Once the request is accepted, you can invite them to a World of Chel dressing room or an Online Versus lobby.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimized Connection">
For the best crossplay experience, using a wired LAN connection is highly recommended to reduce latency (ping) when playing against users on different networks.
</Callout>
